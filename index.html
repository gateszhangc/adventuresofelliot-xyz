Story

A Tale Across Millennia

Long ago, the world was woven together by the Millennium Tales — ancient chronicles that bound the fate of all civilizations. When a dark force began erasing these tales from existence, the balance of time itself started to unravel.

You play as Elliot, a young chronicler chosen by the last keeper of the tales. Armed with the Millennium Compass, you must travel across eras, explore forgotten ruins, solve ancient puzzles, and restore the stories before they fade forever.

From the floating islands of Aetheria to the sunken city of Marath, every realm holds a piece of the lost tales. The journey will test your courage, your wits, and your heart.
